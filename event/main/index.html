Main Events

Field Camp 2021 main event activities is field session and will be carried out for 6 days on March 8-13, 2021 in the Gadjah Mada University area where the point of acquisition has been determined according to the applicable health protocol. In this activity, a geophysical method acquisition process will be carried out from the first to the sixth day. Participants will be divided into 5 groups each day using different methods. Then processing and interpretation activities will be carried out online for 6 days on March 15-20th 2021.
