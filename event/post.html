Post Events

After field session in Field Camp UGM 2021, there will be 2 social activities and final report

Field Camp
Scholarship 2021

This program is a scholarship provision in the form of an allowance for academic needs for our undergraduate students (Geophysics Students 2018, 2019 and 2020) who need or have academic achievements. The scholarship will be made at the end of the field camp sessions.

Distribution of
Masks and Hand Sanitizers

This Field Camp activity will be carried out in the covid-19 pandemic conditions, which next year cannot be predicted. So that this program is carried out to help reduce the spread of covid-19 and implement a culture of using masks and always washing hands according to health protocols. The distribution will be carried out in the area around the Gadjah Mada University campus.

Final Report
Presentation

In this session, the survey results of each method on Field Camp 2021 will be presented. The final report of the Field Camp activity is made in the form of a paper that will refine until the final presentation is held. The presenter will explain the results and conclusions of the study. This event will be held in the fourth week of March 2021.
