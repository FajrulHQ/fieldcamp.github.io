Geophysical
Field Camp 2021

Field camp 2021 is a geophysical field project which is specially planned and executed by senior students of Geophysics Sub Department Universitas Gadjah Mada. This event is organized by Society of Exploration Geophysicist (SEG). Field camp 2021 integrates geophysical methods in a thematic project related to mineral prospecting in an area.

This Project has given a great amount of beneficial experiences in understanding a series of geophysical projects. From Surveying the field to presenting the result will be done systematically. Geophysics field cam activity is set identically with real geophysical project. The participant will work in a team to manage the project which requires a preparation stage; geological fieldtrip, HSE Training, and class-session; the conduction of the project itself which includes data acquisition, processing, and interpretation; and reporting which includes final presentation and result publication.

The project will contribute benefits not only to geophysics community, but also to the society resides around Field Camp area by giving outdoor education concerning natural resources and hazard potential that exist in the area. Moreover, we will be holding a social project as our dedication to society.
